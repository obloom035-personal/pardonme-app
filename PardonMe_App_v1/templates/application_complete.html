Your Pardon Application is Complete

You have successfully completed all seven sections of your Wisconsin pardon application. Your information has been collected and is ready for final preparation.

Next Steps:

  1. Download Your Application: Click the button below to generate and download your completed pardon application as a PDF.
  2. Gather Required Documents: Collect certified copies of your criminal complaint, information, and judgment of conviction from the clerk of courts.
  3. Review Everything: Carefully review your completed application and all supporting documents.
  4. Mail Your Application: Send your complete application packet to the address below.
  5. Wait for Response: Due to high demand, it may take over a year for action on your application.

Mail your completed application to:

Office of the Governor
Attn: Pardon Advisory Board
P.O. Box 7863
Madison, WI 53707

Important: Keep a copy of your application for your records. You must notify the Pardon Advisory Board if your contact information changes.

For questions about the pardon process, visit: evers.wi.gov/Pages/pardon-information.aspx
